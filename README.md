## 🖥️ About MDviewer

MDviewer is a straightforward application for viewing Markdown documents. It works on macOS and shows your Markdown files with clean fonts and layouts. You can also print your documents or save them as PDFs directly from the app. This tool helps you read and share Markdown without the need for extra software or coding.

- At least 2 GB of free RAM

- A screen resolution of 1280x720 or higher

## 📚 Using MDviewer to View Markdown Files

### Step 1: Open a Markdown File

- After launching MDviewer, you have two ways to open your Markdown file:
  - Drag the `.md` file directly onto the MDviewer window.
  - Or click `File` > `Open` and select your file from the dialog.

### Step 2: Read Your Document

- Your document will display with clean typography and easy-to-read layout.
- Links, images, and headers appear as expected.
- You can scroll or resize the window to fit your comfort.

### Step 3: Export as PDF

- To save your document as a PDF:
  - Click `File` > `Export as PDF`.
  - Choose your destination folder.
  - Click `Save`.
- Your Markdown will export as a PDF that looks like a printed page.

### Step 4: Print Your Document

- To print directly, click `File` > `Print`.
- Adjust printer settings if needed.
- Click `Print` to start printing.

## 🔧 Troubleshooting and Tips

- If MDviewer won’t open, check your Security & Privacy settings under `System Preferences`. You may need to allow apps from unidentified developers.
- For slow file loading, make sure your Mac has enough free memory and close unused applications.
- Large images may slow down rendering. Use optimized images if possible.
- PDFs keep fonts and styles consistent across devices.
- If you accidentally delete your files, they will not be recovered by MDviewer. Always keep backups.
- You can open multiple Markdown files by running several MDviewer windows.
